Game Rules
Objective, card values, round flow
›
🎯 Objective

Beat the dealer with a hand sum lower than theirs. If the dealer's value crosses 32 they bust and all staying players win their front bet.

🃏 Card Values
  • Ace = 1
  • 2–9 = face value
  • 10, J, Q, K = 10
0️⃣ Zero Rules (cards count as 0)
  • 3 of a kind
  • 4 of a kind
  • Straight flush of 3+ same suit
⏱️ Round Flow
  • 10s — Front Bet (+ optional Tie Bet)
  • 4 cards dealt; dealer shows the 4th
  • 10s — Push (fold, lose front) or Bet (back bet = 2× front)
  • 5th card dealt; hands revealed
