Banker DQ payout — what I found
Looking at poker-server/PokerRoom.js, there are two paths where the banker is disqualified, and both pay flat 2× regardless of player specials:

A player who legitimately declared Royal Flush (7×), 6½ (8×), Full Suit (10×), SSS/FFF (5×), or Four of a Kind / Straight Flush (3×) is being underpaid when the banker is DQ'd. They also lose the special bonus that the house normally awards (getSpecialBonus() — ranges $1K–$50K standard, $100K–$3M VIP).
Proposed fix:
For each non-DQ player with a bet > 0:
  multiplier = max(2, player.declaredSpecial?.multiplier ?? 2)
  payout    = bet × multiplier         ← from banker (or wallet-debt if banker bust)
  bonus     = player.declaredSpecial ? getSpecialBonus(name, isVip) : 0   ← from house
  player.chips += payout + bonus
Bonus is paid by the house (consistent with resolveRound's normal path). Multiplier portion comes from the banker even if it pulls his wallet negative — same wallet-debt mechanism that already exists for bust players will then charge his bank.
