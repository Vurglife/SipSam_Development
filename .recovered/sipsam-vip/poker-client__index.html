Payouts & Specials
💰 Round Payouts (per bet placed)
Win all 3 hands+2× bet
Win 2 of 3 hands+1× bet
Win 1 of 3 hands−1× bet
Lose all 3 hands−2× bet

Tie hands count for the Banker (except suited flush ties).

⭐ Specials — Multiplier + House Bonus

Declare BEFORE arranging. Winner receives multiplier × bet from loser PLUS the house bonus shown below — bonus is paid by the house, not by other players.

SPECIAL MULT BONUS

Both players keep their own special's bonus from the house, regardless of who wins the round.

⚠️ Disqualification Costs
  • Miss countdown timer → lose round bet
  • Wrong special declared → lose round bet
  • Player hides a real special → lose bet + pay Banker 2× bet
  • Banker hides a real special → pay every player 2× their bet
Quick Rules
♠ Hand Ranking (Strongest → Weakest)
  1. Royal Flush
  2. Straight Flush
  3. Four of a Kind
  4. Full House
  5. Flush (beats Straight in SipSam)
  6. Straight
  7. Three of a Kind
  8. Two Pair
  9. One Pair
  10. High Card
🃏 Hand Arrangement
  • 1st — 3 cards — weakest — furthest from you
  • 2nd — 5 cards — middle strength
  • 3rd — 5 cards — strongest — closest to you
💰 Payouts
  • Win all 3 hands → +2× bet
  • Win 2 of 3 → +1× bet
  • Win 1 of 3 → −1× bet
  • Lose all 3 → −2× bet
⭐ Specials (override all hands)

Must be declared before arranging. Winner gets multiplier × bet plus a house bonus. Bonus is paid once by the house — not charged to any player.

SPECIAL MULT BONUS
No Face 2× +$1,000
Four of a Kind 3× +$2,500
Straight Flush 3× +$2,500
Straight-Straight-Straight 5× +$7,000
Flush-Flush-Flush 5× +$7,000
Royal Flush 7× +$10,000
6½ Pairs 8× +$15,000
Full Suit 10× +$50,000

Straight Flush = 5+ consecutive cards of same suit across all 13 cards.

🚫 Tie Rules
  • Banker wins all ties except flush ties
  • Flush ties: Hearts > Spades > Diamonds > Clubs
⚠️ Disqualifications
  • Miss timer → lose round bet
  • Wrong Special → lose round bet
  • Banker hides Special → pays all 2× bet
  • Player hides Special → loses bet + pays Banker 2×
