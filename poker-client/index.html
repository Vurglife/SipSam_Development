Quick Rules
♠ Hand Ranking (Strongest → Weakest)
  1. Royal Flush
  2. Straight Flush
  3. Four of a Kind
  4. Full House
  5. Flush (beats Straight in SipSam)
  6. Straight
  7. Three of a Kind
  8. Two Pair
  9. One Pair
  10. High Card
🃏 Hand Arrangement
  • 1st — 3 cards — weakest — furthest from you
  • 2nd — 5 cards — middle strength
  • 3rd — 5 cards — strongest — closest to you
💰 Payouts
  • Win all 3 hands → +2× bet
  • Win 2 of 3 → +1× bet
  • Win 1 of 3 → −1× bet
  • Lose all 3 → −2× bet
⭐ Specials (override all hands)
  • No Face — all 13 cards are non-face cards — 2 to 1
  • Four of a Kind — any hand has four of same rank — 3 to 1
  • Straight Flush — any hand has 5 consecutive same suit — 3 to 1
  • Straight-Straight-Straight — all 3 hands are straights — 5 to 1
  • Flush-Flush-Flush — all 3 hands are flushes — 5 to 1
  • Royal Flush — any hand has A-K-Q-J-10 same suit — 7 to 1
  • 6½ Pairs — 6 matching pairs among 13 cards — 8 to 1
  • Full Suit — all 13 cards same suit — 10 to 1
🚫 Tie Rules
  • Banker wins all ties except flush ties
  • Flush ties: Hearts > Spades > Diamonds > Clubs
⚠️ Disqualifications
  • Miss timer → lose round bet
  • Wrong Special → lose round bet
  • Banker hides Special → pays all 2× bet
  • Player hides Special → loses bet + pays Banker 2×
