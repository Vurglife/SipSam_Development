Blackjack Rules
📋 Basic Rules
  • Beat dealer — get closer to 21 without going over
  • Blackjack (A + 10-value) pays 3:2
  • Dealer stands on soft 17
  • Double down on any first 2 cards
  • Split matching pairs — play as 2 separate hands
  • Split Aces: one card each, auto-stand
  • Insurance: fixed $50 bet, pays 2:1 if dealer has Blackjack
🎯 Tie Bet
  • Place $100 during betting phase
  • Win $2,000 Bonus if your total equals dealer's total
  • Works even if both player and dealer bust with same total
  • $500 main bet earns $3,000 Bonus on a tie
  • Independent of round result — you can lose the round and still win the bonus
💰 Payouts
  • Blackjack: 3:2
  • Win: 1:1
  • Push: bet returned
  • Insurance: 2:1
Optional Bets
  • Tie Bet is optional each round
  • Freeze Tie repeats your Tie Bet Yes/No choice until changed
